Bal Ashram and Amrit Yoga

Nurturing young minds and bodies through yoga and education

Bal Ashram serves as a loving home for children who have been orphaned, abandoned, or come from underprivileged backgrounds. Here, children receive not only shelter and education but also love, care, and spiritual guidance. The ashram provides a nurturing environment where children can grow physically, mentally, and spiritually while learning the ancient wisdom of yoga and meditation.

Amrit Yoga is the spiritual practice component of our work, integrating physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation to create a holistic approach to wellness. Children and adults alike benefit from these teachings, which promote flexibility, strength, mental clarity, and inner peace.

Our programs are designed to be accessible to all, regardless of age or experience level. We believe that yoga is for everyone and that the benefits of practice should be available to all who seek them. Through regular practice, students develop not only physical health but also emotional balance and spiritual awareness.
