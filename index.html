About Amrit Sagar

A spiritual home on the sacred Ganges

Amrit Sagar is a spiritual retreat center located in the sacred city of Varanasi, India. Our center offers a unique opportunity to experience authentic ashram life while practicing yoga, meditation, and selfless service in the tradition of Aghor.

Nestled on the banks of the holy Ganges River, our center provides a peaceful environment for spiritual growth, personal transformation, and community living.
