Day Visit

Experience the Ashram for a Day

Experience a day at Amrit Sagar Ashram and immerse yourself in the tranquil atmosphere of our spiritual community. where ancient wisdom meets modern living on the sacred banks of the Ganges.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat from city life or simply want to experience authentic ashram living, our day visit program offers a perfect introduction to our community and practices.

  • Peaceful environment on the Ganges
  • Authentic spiritual experience
  • Interaction with community members
  • Vegetarian meals included
