Our Holistic Farm

Sustainable agriculture and organic living practices

Amrit Sagar Holistic Farm is a pioneering initiative that combines sustainable agriculture with spiritual wisdom. Located on the outskirts of Varanasi, our farm serves as a living laboratory where ancient agricultural practices meet modern ecological consciousness. We believe that caring for the earth is an essential part of spiritual practice, and that growing food sustainably is an offering of love to the planet.

Our farm produces organic vegetables, fruits, and herbs using traditional methods that have been passed down through generations. We practice crop rotation, natural pest control, and water conservation techniques that protect the ecosystem while producing nutrient-rich, healthy food for our ashram community and the wider Varanasi area.

The Holistic Farm is more than just an agricultural project â€“ it's a place of learning and service. Here, volunteers and community members come together to work the land, learn about sustainable living, and participate in the ancient practice of karma yoga through mindful work. The farm provides fresh, organic produce for our kitchen and serves as an educational resource for children and adults alike.

Water Conservation

Crop Rotation

Diverse crop rotation to maintain soil health and prevent pest buildup.

Composting

Natural composting methods to enrich soil and reduce waste.
